Services

  • Medford Tour (Only Medford)

    Tour Length:  2 hours

    Price:  $20 per person

    Enjoy a nice ride within our Medford community, learning about our history throughout time, our growth in our neighborhoods and education, amazing landmarks, and much, much more. You will come out of your trip amazed at how wonderful and peaceful Medford truly is and why make this our home.

  • Medford & Beyond (Medford & Greater Boston Area)

    Tour Length:  4-5 hours

    Price:  $45 per person

    Not only do you get to enjoy Medford, you can also join us in viewing the wonders of other cities outside of Medford like Boston. Come join us in a much extensive journey of Medford, Somerville, Boston, and even more locations.

  • Coffee, Chat, and Chill

    Tour Length:  For as long as you want to!!!

    Price:  $15 per hour

    Meet the residents of the community and have a nice chat with what their lives are like here in Medford. Along with a nice chat, we also provide you with great food service throughout, starting with breakfast all the way through dinner. We make sure you feel and taste our community and its kindness.
